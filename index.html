Activities

Fuertaventura has round twenty six surf spots dedicated to beginers advanced or experience surfers. Most of which are located on north and north west of the island.
For those interested in underwater life, there is many places find many scooba centers with instructors and lessons to get started.
Volcanic moonlike view on island creates many oportunities to hike volcanos and enjoing views around volanos from top of it as well as volcanos itslef.
Small island with lagune inside, untill 1970 only lithhouse man family lived on. Great destination for snorkling in peace and quiet.
Located on north east of the island dunes are breath taking place to see. Dunes lying close to coast, so we can enjoy them same time spending time close to sea.
Want to try kitesurfing . Along with constant wind, and great shallow water cost line of jandia beach in midle of island, lies great place to try kitesurfing.

Best places to eat.

VREBAC ICECREAM

There is probably no words to describe Vrebac icecream bar. Home made organic icecream created by passioned and open hearted Alexander. Made with care for every detail to serve customers lovely icecreams with uniqe tastes. Clasic icecream, bubble waffles, or custom piece of art icecream with topings of our chocie, everything waites on the main street of Corralejo at Vrebac icecream bar.


LA JAIRA DE DEMIAN

Run by the ever-helpful and astonishingly polite Demian, this excellent restaurant enjoys a lovely setting with an outside terrace, next to a deserted plot that La Jaira has converted into a garden.Expect superb tapas and, on the menú del día, such treats as creamy vegetable soup, delightful steamed hake and, for postre, yummy banana purée. Dishes arrive with three different varieties of olive oil and salt. Top notch..


A PONIENTE GASTRO BAR

"The cuisine in A Poniente is a funny one, with «tapas», ideal to share. But we also offer the possibility to enjoy main and more elaborated dishes. Let’s say that it’s a classical cuisine with some personal touches.."
